Framework

Hatch works closely with disadvantaged youths, high-potential startups, and training providers.

  1. Hatch selects youths referred from welfare organizations by motivation and interest.
  2. Hatch trains youths using an industry-relevant curriculum provided by companies.
  3. Hatch places youths in partner companies, where they have the potential to be hired full-time after 3 months.
