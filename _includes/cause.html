Malformed meritocracy

Our society's overemphasis of paper qualifications makes it nigh impossible for at-risk youths to explore and pursue areas they have passion and potential in.

Opportunity gap

Opportunities in other aspects of career preparation, such as internships and career advice, are not readily accessible to youths of lower socio-economic groups.

Lagging workforce

The nature of jobs is changing rapidly. Many employers have difficulty finding sufficiently skilled labour to meet operational needs.
