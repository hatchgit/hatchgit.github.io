Our Work

Let's try this out!

Hatch provides effective training and job matching services to the youths who need it most by working closely with our industry partners.

  1. Identify potential industry entry points for participants
  2. Design training to meet the needs of both participants and industry
  3. Screen participants for programme suitability
  4. Participants undertake and complete training
  5. Transition to on-the-job training and employment at partner firms
